The Grand Washington Hotel is the first [main mission](/missions.html) you encounter, which is also replayable. Located in Down Town east, it's a very vertical encounter as you battle your way up to the top of the hotel.  

Game Description: Rendezvous with Agent Kelso and stage a daring assault to rescue Eleanor Sawyer from the Hyenas.

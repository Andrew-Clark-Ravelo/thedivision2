--- title: Factions in The Division 2 meta: description: Various factions inhabit the world of The Division 2. Meet them here and find out what you to look out for with them. ---

DC Is a dangerous place. Besides The Division, Civilians have made their settlements and communities as well as the JTF has set up the base of operations in the White House.

The city is inhabited by four dangerous factions that wont give up their choke hold on the city so easy.

Hyenas

Rowdy group of decentralized pockets. They basically go for the quick and easy kills and are not very organized.

True Sons

A paramilitary group consisting of JTF mutineers and other sympathizers.

Outcasts

Led by Emeline Shaw. Came from a failed quarantine at Roosevelt Island and are out for vengeance.

Black Tusk

The Black Tusks show up after you have completed the main storyline of The Division 2 and invade all the main missions, giving you extra challenges to beat them again. They are well organized, trained, armed to the teeth and carry a lot of protection.

Welcome to Washington DC, Agent

After the events in New York of the first Division, the dollar flu has spread across the country. You arrive in the city six months after the events in New York have taken place, to a summery scenery of desolation. You and up to three other agents are now tasked to free the people in the city of the various factions that have popped up. Equipped with weapons and gear, as well as your special SHD skills, roam the map and help to protect what remains.

Here you can find all sorts of useful info that will make your journey easier. You can find them all under the info menu at the top. Good luck Agent, you're going to need it.

Year one pass

The year one pass contains a few perks for players who own it.

  • 7 day early access to narrative content of Episodes 1 - 3
  • 8 classified assignments (Investigate the fall of DC)
  • Instant unlock to the 3 specializations as they become available (Other players need to complete something to get them)
  • 3 Backpack charms
  • Agent Ward outfit
  • Scout Emote
  • Additional bounties (weekly) and projects (daily)
